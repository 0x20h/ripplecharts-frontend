Manage Gateways

Customize and manage currencies and gateways for the drop down in the ‘Live Chart’ page. For gateways that are not already in the drop down, use the “Add gateways manually” field to add a custom gateway based on its ripple address or name.

Customizations such as gateway currency pairs and custom gateways are saved in cookies locally. Removing the cookies or accessing ripplecharts.com on a different browser will not have the customizations transferred over.
